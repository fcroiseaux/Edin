How the scaling-law model works ▾
Rewards follow a power-law relationship across time horizons: longer-term engagement yields disproportionately greater rewards relative to the sum of shorter cycles. This means your weekly rewards are good — but your monthly rewards are significantly more than 4x your weekly, and your quarterly rewards are significantly more than 3x your monthly. The compounding effect rewards sustained, high-quality engagement. The longer you stay and contribute quality work, the steeper your reward curve becomes.
